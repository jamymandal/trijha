Who are We?

Trijha, an initiative by Enactus IIT Delhi, provides eco-friendly Home Gardening Kits and organic gardening products. We are committed to sustainability, community empowerment, and healthcare, creating a direct positive impact on the tribal communities of Jhabua, Madhya Pradesh, while promoting greener living practices for all.
